ABOUT ME

H E L L O

I am a retail pharmacist trying to pivot into the world of software engineering! I’ve been in retail pharmacy for eight years, and most of my working days were spent in high-volume, high-performing stores. In those settings I learned to be flexible under stress, to prioritize, to delegate, and communicate! While I love the accessibility of healthcare in the retail setting, I found myself taking a coding course towards the tail end of COVID-19, and enjoying those courses immensely! I loved the problem solving, and I loved learning a new thing. I spent my free weeknights and weekends trying to learn whatever I could, and eventually decided to pull the trigger to take it another step further via a bootcamp - Rithm. I’m excited to see how I’ll grow and learn, and for the unknown but full-of-possibilities future ahead.
